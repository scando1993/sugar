# Feature Brainstorming

## Iron Law
`DIVERGE BEFORE YOU CONVERGE — NO FILTERING DURING IDEATION`

## Phases

### 1. Diverge
Generate 10+ ideas without judgment. Quantity over quality. Wild ideas welcome. Write every idea down, no matter how impractical.

### 2. Cluster
Group related ideas into themes. Name each cluster. Look for patterns and connections.

### 3. Evaluate
Score each cluster: feasibility (1-5), impact (1-5), effort (1-5). Rank by impact/effort ratio.

### 4. Converge
Select top 3 ideas. For each: 2-sentence description, 3 key risks, one concrete next step.

## Red Flags

| Thought | Reality |
|---|---|
| "This idea won't work because..." | You're filtering during diverge. Write it down anyway. |
| "We only need 3-4 ideas" | 10+ minimum. The best ideas come after the obvious ones. |
| "Let me evaluate as I go" | Separate diverge from converge. Evaluate AFTER generating. |
| "This is too wild/impractical" | Wild ideas spark practical ones. Capture everything. |

## Rules
- Generate at least 10 ideas before evaluating any
- No criticism during diverge phase
- Each idea gets equal initial weight
- Output a ranked list with concrete next steps
